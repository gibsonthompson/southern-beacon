Commercial Mold Services

Documented assessment, phased remediation that keeps you operating, and verification records that hold up with tenants, insurers, and counsel.

Different Stakes, Same Standard

In a commercial building the mold is rarely the whole problem. There is a tenant with a complaint on record, an insurer asking questions, a lease with habitability language, and a business that cannot simply close for a week. The technical work follows the same ANSI/IICRC S520 standard as any residential project. What changes is the sequencing, the documentation, and how carefully the work is kept away from occupied space.

Who We Work With

  • Property managers and landlords responding to tenant complaints under Georgia habitability obligations
  • Office and retail tenants dealing with building envelope or HVAC-driven moisture
  • Multifamily owners with unit-to-unit or common area contamination
  • Healthcare, childcare, and senior living facilities where occupant sensitivity raises the bar
  • Restaurants and food service where kitchen humidity and drain issues drive growth
  • Facilities teams needing an independent assessment before litigation or a claim

Working Around Your Operation

Most commercial projects are phased so the building keeps running. That means containment that isolates the work zone from occupied areas, HVAC isolation so nothing travels through shared ductwork, after-hours and weekend scheduling where daytime work would be disruptive, and clear communication to building occupants about what is happening and why.

Documentation That Survives Scrutiny

Commercial mold situations frequently end up in front of someone other than you: an adjuster, an attorney, a tenant's expert, or a regulator. We document from first walk-through through final clearance, with photographs, moisture logs, sample chain of custody, accredited laboratory reports, and written clearance determinations. Whatever the outcome, the record shows what the conditions were and what was done about them.

Assessment Only, If That Is What You Need

Some clients need a defensible assessment and nothing else, particularly during a dispute where the assessing party should not also be bidding the removal. We perform assessment-only engagements and provide the report without a remediation proposal attached. Say so up front and that is how we will scope it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you work after hours?+
Yes. Most occupied commercial projects are scheduled evenings, weekends, or in phases so the space stays usable during business hours.
Do you handle tenant communication?+
We provide the technical documentation and plain-language explanations you can share with occupants. Direct tenant communication stays with ownership or management unless you ask us to participate.
Can you provide an assessment without a remediation bid?+
Yes. Assessment-only engagements are available and are often the right call when a dispute or claim is involved.
