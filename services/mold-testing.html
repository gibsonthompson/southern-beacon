Know Exactly What You're Dealing With

You cannot identify a mold species by looking at it, and you cannot see the moisture moving inside a wall. Professional testing takes the guesswork out. Whether you have spotted visible growth, noticed a persistent musty odor, or received a home inspection report that flagged moisture, an inspection tells you what is actually happening in your home.

Our inspections combine a thorough visual evaluation with moisture meter readings and, where appropriate, air and surface sampling. All samples are analyzed by an accredited laboratory, and you receive a written report explaining the findings in plain language.

What an Inspection Includes

  • Complete visual inspection of the areas of concern and surrounding spaces
  • Moisture meter readings to locate hidden dampness in walls and floors
  • Air samples comparing indoor spore levels to outdoor baseline levels
  • Surface samples of visible growth for species identification
  • Accredited laboratory analysis of all samples
  • A written report with findings, photos, and clear recommendations

When Testing Makes Sense

Testing is most valuable when mold is suspected but not visible, when you need to identify the species for medical or legal reasons, during a real estate transaction, or after remediation to confirm the work succeeded. If mold is clearly visible and the source is obvious, we may recommend moving straight to remediation and putting your budget toward the fix instead. We will always tell you honestly which situation you are in.

Independent, Honest Results

Because we provide both testing and remediation, we hold ourselves to a strict standard: the test results speak for themselves. If your results come back normal, we tell you no work is needed. A significant portion of our inspections end exactly that way, and we consider that a good outcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does mold testing cost?+
Inspection is free. If laboratory sampling is appropriate for your situation, we quote the sampling cost upfront before collecting anything. You will never receive a surprise lab bill.
How long do results take?+
Standard laboratory turnaround is two to three business days. Expedited analysis is available when a real estate closing or health concern requires faster answers.
What if the test finds mold?+
You receive a written remediation estimate along with your results. There is no obligation to use us for the work, and your report is yours to take to any contractor.
