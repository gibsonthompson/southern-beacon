Permanent solutions for wet basements: interior drainage, wall sealing, and sump systems engineered for Georgia clay and backed by a written warranty.

Stop Water at the Source

Metro Atlanta sits on dense clay soil that absorbs water slowly and holds it against your foundation. After heavy rain, that saturated clay creates hydrostatic pressure that pushes moisture through block walls, floor joints, and cracks. The result is the damp line, the puddle, or the musty basement smell that comes back every time it storms.

Sealing a crack or painting on a waterproof coating treats the symptom. A properly designed waterproofing system manages the water itself, collecting it where it enters and moving it away from your foundation permanently.

Our Waterproofing Solutions

  • Interior drainage systems that collect water at the footing and channel it to a sump
  • Sump pump installation, including battery backup systems for storm outages
  • Foundation wall sealing and vapor barriers
  • Crack repair and structural evaluation
  • Exterior grading and downspout drainage correction
  • Written warranty on installed systems

Designed Around Your Foundation, Not a Package

Every home takes on water differently. Before recommending anything, we evaluate where water actually enters your foundation, how your lot drains, and what your walls are telling us. The system we propose is designed for your house, and the written estimate explains exactly what each component does and why it is included.

Waterproofing and Mold, Handled Together

A chronically damp basement almost always develops mold eventually. Because we handle both waterproofing and remediation, we can address existing growth and the water source in a single project. One contractor, one plan, one warranty.
