When Water Gets In, Hours Matter

A burst pipe, a failed water heater, or storm flooding puts water into materials that were never meant to be wet. Drywall wicks moisture upward within hours. Wood framing absorbs it. And in Georgia's climate, mold growth can begin within 24 to 48 hours of a water event. The faster water is extracted and drying begins, the less damage your home sustains and the less the repair ultimately costs.

We answer the phone 24 hours a day, and emergency calls in metro Atlanta are dispatched immediately.

Our Emergency Response Includes

  • Immediate dispatch for active water emergencies, day or night
  • Water extraction with commercial equipment
  • Removal of unsalvageable wet materials
  • Structural drying with commercial air movers and dehumidifiers
  • Daily moisture readings logged until the structure reads dry
  • Complete photo and moisture documentation for your insurance claim

Dry Means Verified Dry

Surfaces can feel dry while the framing behind them holds enough moisture to grow mold. We monitor with moisture meters throughout the drying process and continue until readings confirm the structure has actually returned to normal levels. You receive the drying logs as part of your documentation.

Working With Your Insurance

Water damage claims move faster and settle better with thorough documentation. We photograph conditions from first arrival, log every reading, and provide the records your adjuster needs. If your situation may involve a covered loss, our documentation gives you the strongest possible position.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you get here?+
Emergency calls in metro Atlanta are dispatched immediately, 24 hours a day, including weekends and holidays.
Should I start cleaning up before you arrive?+
If it is safe, shut off the water source and electricity to affected areas, and move valuables out of the water. Leave extraction and drying to us; consumer equipment cannot dry structural materials adequately.
Will insurance cover the damage?+
Sudden events like burst pipes are often covered. Coverage depends on your policy and the cause, and our documentation supports your claim either way.
