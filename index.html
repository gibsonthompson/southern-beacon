Common Questions

Before You Call

How fast can you get here?+
We answer 24/7. For active water emergencies in metro Atlanta we dispatch immediately. Inspections and waterproofing estimates are usually scheduled within 24 to 48 hours.
Is the inspection really free?+
Yes. We inspect, explain what we found, and give you a written price. If you don't need remediation, we tell you that too. No charge, no obligation either way.
Is black mold dangerous?+
Color alone doesn't identify a mold species. Some molds aggravate allergies and asthma, especially for kids, seniors, and anyone with respiratory issues. Lab testing tells you exactly what's growing so you know whether it's a health concern or just a stain.
Can I clean mold with bleach?+
Scrubbing or spraying visible mold sends spores into the air and does nothing about the moisture that grew it, so it comes back. Proper remediation means containment, HEPA air filtration, removing affected materials, and fixing the water source.
Does insurance cover mold removal?+
It depends on the cause. Sudden covered events like a burst pipe often qualify. Long-term leaks and groundwater usually don't. We document everything with photos, moisture readings, and reports so you have the strongest case with your carrier.
What does mold remediation cost in Atlanta?+
Small contained jobs can run a few hundred dollars. Larger jobs with demolition, structural drying, and lab testing cost more. You get a written itemized price after inspection, before any work starts.
How do I know the mold is actually gone?+
We run post-remediation clearance air testing through an accredited lab and hand you the results. You get documented proof the air is back to normal.
Will the water come back after waterproofing?+
Not if the system matches how water actually moves around your foundation. We evaluate grading, drainage, and entry points before recommending anything, and our waterproofing work is backed by a written warranty.
